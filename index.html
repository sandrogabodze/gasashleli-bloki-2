Basketball basketball is a team sport in which two teams, commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete with the primary
objective of shooting a basketball (approximately 9,4 inches(24cm) in diameter) through the defender's hoop(a basket 18 inches(46cm)in diameter mounted
10feet(3.048 m) high to a backboard at each end of the court).

Football Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. Unqualified, the word football normally means the form of football that is the most popular where the word is used.
